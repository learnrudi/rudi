Overview

AI readiness partner for state coordination hubs and enterprise AI adoption

RUDI partners with state coordination hubs, federal subaward programs, and enterprise organizations to deliver AI readiness from literacy to fluency. Our 3-tier certification and 30-Point AI Competency Framework map directly to NSF's own definition of AI-Ready — a framework we built before NSF named theirs.

Since 2023, RUDI has delivered AI training, consulting, and deployment support to adult professionals across education, enterprise, logistics, built environment, philanthropy, and community organizations. Our audience is educators, workforce leaders, and public-sector staff — the people who in turn teach, lead, and serve their own constituencies. We are SAM.gov registered and ready to serve as a named partner or subawardee on NSF 26-508 Round 1 coalitions and other state AI coordination initiatives.

Framework Alignment

RUDI's curriculum maps to NSF's AI-Ready continuum

NSF's internal definition of AI-Ready is a three-node continuum: Literacy → Proficiency → Fluency. RUDI's 3-tier certification, built in 2023, maps to the exact same continuum.

NSF Term NSF Definition RUDI Course
Literacy Ability to understand — answers "why AI?" and "when AI?" → Level 1: AI Literacy · 8 hrs
Proficiency Ability to apply AI in real workflows → Level 2: Applied AI · 16 hrs
Fluency Ability to create with AI — build systems, agents, automations → Level 3: AI Assistants, Agents & Workflows · 24 hrs
