# RUDI — Capabilities Statement

**Responsible Use of Digital Intelligence**
AI Readiness Partner for State Coordination Hubs, Federal Subaward Programs, and Enterprise Adoption

---

## Core Competencies

- **AI Training & Certification** — 3-tier curriculum (Literacy → Applied → Agents & Workflows); 30-Point AI Competency Framework; stackable credentials; TechCred-eligible.
- **AI Deployment Support** — Hands-on adoption coaching, assessment frameworks, tool selection, implementation support, workflow playbooks.
- **Navigator Content Strategy** — Taxonomy design, curriculum integration, and content curation for statewide AI resource platforms.
- **Curriculum Development** — K-16 AI curriculum aligned to DOL AI Literacy Framework, WIOA, and Perkins V.
- **Workforce AI Readiness** — Enterprise, public sector, and community organization AI adoption at scale.

## Alignment to NSF AI-Ready America Framework

RUDI's 3-tier certification was built before NSF named its framework. It maps directly to NSF's internal definition of AI-Ready:

| NSF AI-Ready Continuum | RUDI Curriculum |
|---|---|
| **Literacy** — ability to *understand* (why AI? when AI?) | Level 1: AI Literacy (Foundation) |
| **Proficiency** — ability to *apply* | Level 2: Applied AI |
| **Fluency** — ability to *create with* | Level 3: AI Assistants, Agents & Workflows |
