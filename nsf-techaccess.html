Coalition Role

Where RUDI fits in your coalition

Channel 4 — Primary

Training & Certification

RUDI's literacy → proficiency → fluency curriculum matches NSF's own definition of AI-Ready. 30-Point Framework. TechCred-eligible in Ohio. Stackable credentials from foundation through Applied AI through AI Agents & Workflows. Aligned to DOL AI Literacy Framework, WIOA, and Perkins V.

Channel 3 — Primary

Deployment Support

RUDI's Deployment Toolkit scales one coach to serve 3× more organizations. Proven across education, enterprise, and built environment. Assessment, tool selection, implementation, and post-deployment support. Serves SMBs, nonprofits, and public-serving organizations.

Channel 1 — Advisory

Navigator Content Strategy

RUDI partners with platform vendors or university IT to lead taxonomy, curriculum integration, and content curation for statewide AI resource navigators. We don't build the platform — we make sure it works.
